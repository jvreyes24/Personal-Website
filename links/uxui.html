Fil-Am Cuisine: A Redesign Case Study

This case study highlights the website redesign of Fil-Am Cuisine, a Filipino restaurant aiming to enhance its online presence and better reflect its vibrant culinary identity. The project focused on improving user experience, modernizing the visual design, and making it easier for customers to browse the menu, and connect with the restaurant's rich Filipino-American heritage.
